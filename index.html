About

It started with a simple question: why do some posts stop the scroll and most don't? Chasing that answer turned into a real habit — testing hooks, studying what makes people comment instead of just double-tapping, and slowly turning "I think this works" into "I can prove this works."

These days that habit pays the bills two different ways. As a Sales Executive at Tech Cuber, I read what a client actually needs and close the gap between "interested" and "signed." As a marketer for TVA International and TVA – The Virtual Advertisers, I do the same thing for entire brands — content, strategy, campaigns, the whole loop.

The Computer Science degree is the long game. I want to stop being the person who asks a developer for a landing page and start being the person who just builds it.
